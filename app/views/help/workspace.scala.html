What's New?

  • Folders: organize your workspace into folders and sub-folders.
  • Readmes: add descriptive text to your folders to serve as a guide or welcome message.
  • Drag-and-drop import: upload files by simply dragging and dropping them into your workspace. Works for IIIF manifest links, too!
  • Configurable NER: Named Entity Recognition is now available for multiple languages (English, Spanish, French, German - for now), and you can choose the gazetteers Recogito should use for geo-resolution.
  • Customizable document table: got a huge monitor? Or a small tablet? No problem - choose the metadata that appears in the document list to suit your needs and screen size.
  • More table column types: to complement the configurable table view, we added new column types to add to your workspace - such as the Verification Ratio showing the rate of verified vs. unverified annotations.
  • Document grid view: bored with the table view? Check out the our new grid view.
  • Activity Statistics: your recent activity at a glance - as a small chart widget in your workspace sidebar.
  • Collaborator Quicklinks: see who you've been collaborating most recently in your workspace sidebar.
